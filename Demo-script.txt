Summary
BookShelves: 
Web App created for the purpose of managing and storing books by using its title and   book’s cover image. After you become a member of Bookshelves family you have the access to get all books that stored in the database. Then you can add, read, delete and search any book. On the top that, check random serious facts for free account.
